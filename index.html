StepCast

StepCast is a walk tracking website tailored towards podcast listeners with features such as automatic logo fetching from spotify servers. It has both a website and a iOS app yet to release on the app store. StepTrack also has integrated GPS tracking support for both the iOS app and website so you can enjoy hands free walk tracking.
